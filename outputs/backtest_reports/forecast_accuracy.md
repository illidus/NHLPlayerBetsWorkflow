## Metric Definitions
- **Brier Score**: Mean squared error of forecasts. Lower is better (0 to 1).
- **ECE (Expected Calibration Error)**: Average difference between predicted probability and actual frequency. Lower is better.
- **ROC AUC**: Ability to discriminate between hits and misses. Higher is better (0.5 to 1.0).
- **Top-10 Hit Rate**: Percentage of the 10 highest-probability players per slate who actually hit.
- **Gap**: Actual Rate - Avg Model Prob. Positive means under-predicting, negative means over-predicting.
